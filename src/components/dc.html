Aquaman(2018)

Aquaman (2018) is a superhero film based on the DC Comics character Aquaman, directed by James Wan. The film is part of the DC Extended Universe (DCEU) and explores the underwater kingdom of Atlantis and the adventures of its half-human, half-Atlantean hero.

Director: James Wan

Zack Snyder's Justice League(2021)

Zack Snyder's Justice League, often referred to as the "Snyder Cut," is a director's cut of the 2017 superhero film "Justice League." The original 2017 film was directed by Zack Snyder but was heavily altered during post-production due to various reasons. "Zack Snyder's Justice League" was released in 2021 on HBO Max and presents Zack Snyder's original vision for the movie.

Director: Zack Snyder

Cast : Jason Momoa, Amber Heard, William Dafoe, Henry Cavill, Ben Affleck, Gal Gadot.

Produced by: Deborah Snyder, Charles Roven.

Music by: Junkie XL (Tom Holkenborg)
